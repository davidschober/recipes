Cranberry Sauce

Thanksgiving

Prep Time: - | Cook Time: -

Ingredients

  • 1lb. Cranberries
  • 1 orange, diced
  • 1 piece of candied ginger
  • 1 stick of cinnamon
  • 1/3 c. Sugar
  • 1c.  Water

Directions

  • Place all in a saucepan and cover with water.  Bring to boil and cook until cranberries pop. Cool and serve.
