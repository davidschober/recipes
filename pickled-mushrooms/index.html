Pickled Mushrooms

Needs Work

Prep Time: - | Cook Time: - | Servings: -

Ingredients

  • water
  • vinegar
  • button mushrooms

Directions

  • Boil 1/2 water and 1/2 vinegar
  • Add button mushrooms for 5 minutes
  • Drain
  • Cool mushrooms with lemons and peppercorns
  • Place in jars
  • Fill with vinegar
