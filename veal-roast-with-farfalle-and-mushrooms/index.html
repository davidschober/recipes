Veal Roast with Farfalle and Mushrooms

Prep Time: - | Cook Time: - | Servings: -

Ingredients

  • 2-4 T. Olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 lbs veal roast
  • 1-2 cups red wine
  • 1/2 c. Dried porcini mushrooms reconstituted in one cup hot water
  • 8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 t. Thyme
  • 2 T. Butter
  • Salt, pepper
  • 1 lb farfalle pasta

Directions

  • Brown roast on all sides in the oil
  • Add the onion and cook together
  • Add the wine a little at a time
  • Add the porcini and the water, a little at the time
  • Add the thyme
  • Cook for a good hr.
  • When cooked, remove the roast and slice for serving
  • To the pan, add the butter, salt, pepper and fresh mushrooms
  • Add the cooked pasta to the sauce with a little cooked water and more oil if needed
