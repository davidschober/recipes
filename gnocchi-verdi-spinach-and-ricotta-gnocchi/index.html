Gnocchi Verdi: Spinach and Ricotta Gnocchi

Prep Time: - | Cook Time: -

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg frozen, chopped spinach, thawed, cooked (3 min. In micro) and water squeezed out
  • 1 lb. ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 cup flour
  • Salt, pepper, nutmeg

Directions

  • To the cooked spinach, add all other ingredients.
  • Form little balls about the size of a teaspoon
  • Roll on flour sprinkled on a cutting board
  • Cook in plenty of salted water
  • They are done when they come to the top
  • In a pan melt some butter, add a few leaves of sage and the cooked gnocchi
  • Serve with more Parmesan cheese

When done they will rise to the top. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain, meanwhile keep adding the rest of the gnocchi until all cooked. In a saucepan place the butter and sage and melt together.
