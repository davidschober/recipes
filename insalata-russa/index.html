Insalata Russa

Prep Time: - | Cook Time: -

Description

Ingredients

  • 1lb frozen peas
  • 1lb frozen cubed carrots
  • 2 large potatoes boiled and cubed
  • Mayonnaise

Directions

Mix all together. Shape into oval and decorate with Olives, gerghins, capers, anchovies
