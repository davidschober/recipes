Veal and Peas

Prep Time: - | Cook Time: - | Servings: -

Ingredients

  • 1-2 lbs. veal shoulder steak, cut into pieces
  • 2 T. Olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Rosemary, salt, pepper
  • 1/2 c. Wine
  • 1 c. Beef stock
  • 1 lb. frozen peas

Directions

  • Brown meat in the oil, add onion and cook until done
  • Add rosemary, salt and pepper
  • Add wine and when cooked down, add stock a little at a time.
  • Add frozen peas and cook until done
