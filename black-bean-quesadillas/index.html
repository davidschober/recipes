Black bean quesadillas

Dinner

Prep Time: 10 min | Cook Time: 10 min | Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cans black beans
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 poblano peppers, thinly sliced
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp chili (or less, to taste)
  • tortillas
  • shredded cheese

Directions

  • Halve, deseed, and thinly slice poblano crosswise into strips
  • Halve, peel, and thinly slice onion
  • Add olive oil (or butter) to medium pot, medium-high heat
  • Add beans and their liquid
  • Add spices, and big pinch of salt (and pepper if you want)
  • Bring bean mixture to a boil, then reduce to low simmer
  • Cook until thickened, 3-4 minutes.
  • Turn off heat and mash with fork (I do this when heat is still on)
  • Meanwhile, heat some oil in a pan, medium-high heat.
  • Add poblano and onion. Season with salt and pepper
  • Cook, stirring, until softened and lightly charred, 5-7 minutes
  • Assemble quesadillas - thin layer of beans, top with pepper/onion, cheese
  • Pan cook until golden and melty
  • Serve with sour cream, spicy sour cream, avocado, salsa, whatever you want

Notes

  • Can do corn or flour tortillas
  • Can do 2 tortillas or one folded
