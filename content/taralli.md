title = "Taralli"

ingredients = "- 1 cup flour 120 grams\n- 2 tablespoons olive oil 30 grams\n- 4 tablespoons white wine 60 grams\n- 1/2 teaspoon salt 2 1/2 grams\n- 1/2 tablespoon fennel seeds if desired I'm sure sesame seeds would work too"
directions = "- In a large bowl whisk flour, salt and fennel seeds together, add olive oil and wine combine with a wooden spoon, place on a flat surface and knead for approximately 10 minutes until dough is smooth and elastic.\n- Place the dough in a bowl and cover with plastic wrap, let rest for 30 minutes.\n- Pre-heat oven to 400° (200° celsius)\n- Remove dough and roll out into 2-2 1/2 inch ropes and join the ends together to form a tear drop shape. Place shaped drops on a clean tea towel.\n- In a large pot of boiling water, add drops a few at a time (depending on the size of the pot) they will sink, so when they have resurfaced remove with a slotted spoon and place on the tea towel. Then move to a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper.\n- Bake for approximately 20-25 minutes or until golden."
