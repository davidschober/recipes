## Description 

David's No Knead Baguette

## Ingredients 

* 5 cups bread flour
* 1 c. all-purpose flour
* 1/2 tsp yeast
* 2 tsp salt
* 3 1/4 c. water

## Directions 

* 18 hour 1st rise
* 2 hour 2nd rise
* optional 12 hour retard
* Shape 4 loaves
* Bake 2 at a time
* Mist with water before putting in oven
* 475 until done
