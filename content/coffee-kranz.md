directions = "For dough:\n\n* Combine all ingredients in order into one bowl\n* Mix as for pie crust using a pastry cutter\n* Add milk and mix (with hands)\n* Cover and refrigerate over night\n\nCheese filling:\n\n* Cream together ingredients\n* Divide into three portions\n\nAssembly\n\n* On second day, divide dough into three equal parts and roll out fairly thin on a floured surface (12-14 inches)\n* Brush dough with melted butter\n* Spread 1/3 of cheese filling on the buttered dough and roll as a jelly roll\n* Be sure ends are pressed slightly to close and that seam is closed\n* Cover with a cloth and let rise for one hour\n* Bake 30 minutes at 350\n* After baking, cool and dust with powdered sugar\n* To freeze: Wrap in foil and then a plastic bag. These cakes freeze great. Thaw at room temperature. Can also be filled with fruit or almond filling.\n* Good luck"
