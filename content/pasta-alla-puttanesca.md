## Ingredients

* 2 T. oil
* 4 cloves garlic, minced
* 1/4 c. chopped olives
* 1/4 c. capers
* 1 t. red pepper flakes
* 1 can tuna
* 1 can diced tomatoes
* Juice of 1/2 lemon
* zest of lemon
* Parsley
* 1/2 lb pasta
