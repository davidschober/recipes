## Directions

* In a baking pan, place one layer
* 4c. Potatoes sliced thinly
* Meanwhile in a saucepan mix
* 3T. Butter
* 3T. Flour
* 11/2 c. Milk
* 1t. Salt
* Top the first layer with the sauce, top with more potatoes, sauce and
* 1c. Sharp cheddar
* Top with
* 1/2c. Grated cheese
* Paprika
* Bake 350 for 1 hour
