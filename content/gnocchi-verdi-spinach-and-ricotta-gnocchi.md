title = "Gnocchi Verdi: Spinach and Ricotta Gnocchi"
description = "Spinach and ricotta gnocchi with butter and sage sauce"
ingredients = """
* 1 pkg frozen, chopped spinach, thawed, cooked (3 minutes in microwave) and water squeezed out
* 1 lb. ricotta cheese
* 1 egg
* 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
* 3/4 cup flour
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp nutmeg
* 4 T unsalted butter 
* 1T fresh sage (dried can be substituted) """
directions = """
* Mix together spinach, ricotta, egg, nutmeg and salt
* Fold in 4 Tablespoons Parmesan and 1/2 c flour
* Spread 1/4 c flour on a plate
* Drop 1/2 t. of mix and roll around the plate until you have a soft ball the size of a cherry.  
* Place the gnocchi on a lined cookie sheet and cover with plastic wrap until ready to cook.  
* Bring water to a boil and salt it. 
* Drop the gnocchi in 1 by 1 until they cover the bottom of the pot. 
* When done they will rise to the top.  
* Remove with a slotted spoon and drain, meanwhile keep adding the rest of the gnocchi until all cooked. 
* In a saucepan place the butter and sage and melt together.  
* Drizzle on the gnocchi and top with the remaining parmesan."""
