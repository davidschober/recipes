## Ingredients

* 2c. Flour
* 1c. Butter
* 2c. Sugar (1 cup)
* 1 beaten egg
* Dash of salt
* Sliced almonds

## Directions

* Keep half the beaten egg for top
* Knead all ingredients into smooth paste
* Butter a pie pan or spring form pan
* Press dough into it
* Brush with rest of beaten egg
* Press almonds on top
* Bake 30min. at 350
