## Description

Pasta with cream, ham/prosciutto, and peas

## Ingredients

* 1 lb spaghetti
* 1 c. diced ham
* 1 pk. frozen peas
* 1 stick butter
* 1 quart half and half
* 1 c cheese
* salt
* pepper

## Directions

* Melt butter, cook ham and peas in butter
* Add cream
* Cook spaghetti
* Add spaghetti to cream mixture, cook while stirring
* Toss with 1 c. cheese, salt, pepper to taste
