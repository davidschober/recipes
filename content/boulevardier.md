## Ingredients
* 1 - 1.25 part Rye Whiskey (Bourbon for sweeter)
* 1 part Campari
* 1 part Sweet Vermouth

## Directions
* Combine over ice
* Stir
* Serve in chilled glass
* Garnish with orange peel
