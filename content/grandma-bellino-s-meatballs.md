## Ingredients
- 1/2 lb. ground beef
- 1/2 lb. ground pork
- 1 T. Italian seasonings
- 1 egg
- 1/2 c. Grated cheese
- 1/2 c. Breadcrumbs 
- Salt, pepper, diced garlic, chopped parsley

## Directions
- Mix all ingredients together
- Form medium size balls
- Fry or bake
- Add to tomato sauce and cook together
- Serve over pasta
