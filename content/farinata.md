## Ingredients

- 1 C. Garbanzo flour
- 1 C. Water
- 1 t. Salt
- 1 t. Pepper
- 1 t. Rosemary or other herb
- Oil

## Directions
- Whisk flour, water, salt, pepper, herbs and 1 T. Oil
- Refrigerate for 1-2 hrs 
- Preheat a cast iron pan in 450 oven
- Pour 2+T. Oil in pan, add the batter and bake for 5-10min
