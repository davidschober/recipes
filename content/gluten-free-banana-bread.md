title = "Banana Bread - Gluten-free"
ingredients = "* 3 medium ripe bananas (3 bananas yield \\~1 1/2 cups)\n* 1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract\n* 1 whole egg\n* 3 Tbsp avocado or coconut oil, melted\n* 1/4 cup organic cane sugar\n* 1/4 cup packed organic brown sugar\n* 2-3 Tbsp maple syrup (depending on ripeness of bananas // or sub honey)\n* 3 1/2 tsp baking powder\n* 3/4 tsp sea salt\n* 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon\n* 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk (or other dairy-free milk)\n* 1 1/4 cup almond meal\n* 1 1/4 cup gluten-free flour blend\n* 1 1/4 cup gluten-free oats\n* Chocolate chips (optional)"
directions = "* Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and line a 9×5-inch loaf pan with parchment paper.\n* Mash banana in a large bowl. Add all ingredients through almond milk (vanilla, egg, oil, cane sugar, brown sugar, maple syrup, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, almond milk) and whisk vigorously to combine.\n* Last add almond meal, gluten free flour blend and oats and stir.\n* Stir in chocolate chips (optional)\n* Bake for 1 hour – 1 hour 15 minutes. When ready, it should feel firm and be crackly and golden brown on top.\n* Let cool completely before cutting or it will be too tender to hold form.\n* Serve with butter and honey or as is. Store leftovers in a covered container for up to a few days. Slice and freeze for longer term storage."
