## Ingredients 

* 1 T olive oil
* 3 or more onions, sliced
* 1 bouillon cube
* 4 cups water
* 1 T flour (optional)

## Directions 

* Cook onions in oil until brown
* Add flour, bouillon cube, and water
* Bring to boil, then simmer for 1 hour
* Place 1 slice Italian bread topped with Swiss cheese in a bowl
* Top with soup
