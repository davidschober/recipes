## Description 

Grandpa Bellino's wine; Rosa's handwritten notes

## Directions 

* 10 cases muscat
* 2 cases zinfandel
* crush
* place in press
* strain in barrel
* leave 5" from top
* Continue filling with about 1 gal.
* After it boils (1 month)
* Cork it (40 days later)
* After 3-6 months place in bottles
