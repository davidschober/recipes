## Ingredients 

* 1/4-1/2 c. sugar
* 1 c. Bisquick
* 1 egg
* 1/4 c. milk or water
* 1 can peaches

Topping:

* 2 T. Bisquick
* 1 T. butter
* 2 T. brown sugar

## Directions 

* Mix dough
* Add peach slices
* Add topping
* Bake 350, 20-30 minutes

## Notes 

This cobbler is Rosa's recipe, using bisquick. It can be made with other fruit, like blueberries.
